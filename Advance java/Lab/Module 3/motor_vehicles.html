Motor Vehicles in the Indian Market

Below is a list of common vehicle categories found in India, with a short description for each.

Scooter
A two-wheeled motor vehicle with a step-through frame and a platform for the rider's feet. Popular for city commuting due to ease of use and fuel efficiency.
Motorcycle
A two-wheeled motor vehicle with a seating position that usually requires straddling the seat. Common for both commuting and sport riding; comes in many engine sizes.
Hatchback
A compact car with a rear door that opens upward, providing access to the cargo area. Efficient for city driving and easy to park.
Sedan
A 4-door passenger car with a separate trunk (boot). Offers more comfort and often chosen for family use and highway travel.
SUV (Sports Utility Vehicle)
A larger vehicle designed for off-road capability and versatile passenger/cargo space. Popular in India for its commanding driving position and space.
